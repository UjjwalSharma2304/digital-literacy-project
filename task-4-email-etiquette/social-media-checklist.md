# **Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students**

## 

## **The Do's**



* Audit your privacy settings regularly to ensure personal posts are restricted from recruiters and faculty.



* Optimize your LinkedIn profile by consistently sharing technical milestones, project updates, and GitHub links.



* Verify sources before sharing information to prevent the spread of misinformation within your academic network.



* Give proper credit to developers and creators when sharing or "forking" code to maintain high academic integrity.



* Network with clear intent by sending personalized, respectful messages when connecting with industry professionals.



## **The Don'ts**



* Don't post proprietary code or confidential project details from internships, labs, or graded assignments.



* Don't engage in unprofessional public arguments that can permanently damage your digital footprint.



* Don't share sensitive personal data such as your home address, private phone number, or real-time GPS location.



* Don't use unprofessional handles or profile pictures on any platform you use for networking or job hunting.



* Don't forget that "deleted" content is rarely permanent; always consider the long-term impact before posting.
